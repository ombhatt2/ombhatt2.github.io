As a **Hardware Team Member** for **TAMU Robomasters**, I am actively developing the Sentry Robot—a fully autonomous ground robot that moves and shoots at enemy robots using advanced computer vision. 

My primary contributions to the team include:

* Working on making the Sentry robot's design robust by utilizing **OnShape** CAD software.
* Integrating key electronic components such as the **NVIDIA Jetson Orin, LiDAR sensors, and cameras** into the system for real-time perception and decision-making.
