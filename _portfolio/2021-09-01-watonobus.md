As a Research Intern at the **University of Waterloo’s Mechatronics Vehicle Systems Lab**, I designed, fabricated, and programmed an LED display module for the **WATonoBus**, an autonomous shuttle. The module serves as a critical communication interface between the vehicle and non-ego agents (pedestrians and other drivers), visually displaying the shuttle’s autonomous intentions to improve safety in urban environments.
