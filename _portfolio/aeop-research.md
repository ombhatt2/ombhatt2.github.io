During my AEOP Summer Robotics Research Internship at the **UT Austin Oden Institute**, I developed autonomous navigation and control systems for a 1/16 scale RC rally car. I focused on implementing algorithms for high-speed maneuvering, including jump and drift racing, and contributed to research regarding multi-drone formations and coordinate-based autonomous systems.
