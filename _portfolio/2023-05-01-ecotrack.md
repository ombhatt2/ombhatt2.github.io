Developed EcoTrack, a phototropic solar tracker that utilizes thermo-responsive polymers to significantly increase energy harvesting efficiency. This project involved researching material properties and designing a system that mimics natural phototropism to optimize solar panel orientation without the need for complex motorized sensors.
